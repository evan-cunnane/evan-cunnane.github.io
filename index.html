About Me

Hi, My name is Evan Cunnane. I have been creating and producing music since late 2019. I've always loved music and wanted to persue it as a career but never had a voice for it until I found out about the production side and it's been an uncontrollable obsession ever since. I have produced songs for artists all over the world, e.g. America, UK and Ireland. I have learned and used many different softwares such as Ableton Live, FL Studio, Reaper and am currently learning MaxMSP.

I am currently a first year in the University of Limerick, studying Music, Media and Performacne Technology. During my first year in UL I have gained a lot of knowledge and skills in areas such as music, processing, graphic design and web development.

My main focuses right now are on my studies in UL but when I want to take a break from that I am usually in a music studio. I am currently working on an ambient album which will be called "Everything All At Once". Ive been working on this porject with a friend of mine called Shane, who is mainly a guitar player but is a multi instrumentalist. I plann to have this album and hopefully a few B-Sides ready for release by the middle of summer 2025.

People Who Inspire Me

Nick Mira

The first time i discovered music production, it was around 4am i was scrolling on YouTube and I accidently clicked on a video of Nick Mira creating a beat around an artists acapella. I instantly fell in love with what I was seeing. That same day i downloaded FL Studio and began learning everything. The video I watched that night.

Nick Mira taught me the basics of music production, i would religiously listen to everything he produced, watch every live stream he would do and just watch and learn. I studied everything about the instruments and effects he used.

Jonny Greenwood

During my year out my ambition was sky high with music, i had plans to release and produce as much tracks as possible. Unfortunately I hit a major wall, i went from making 10 or so beats a day to maybe finishing 5 in a month. It was a horrific year, i began to think of letting my dream of being a musician go as I felt like I was just doing the same thing over and over. That was until I was properly introduced to one of the greatest bands I've ever hear, Radiohead. Jonny Greenwood is the lead guitarist and multi instrumentalist for Radiohead.

Jonny Greenwood single handly revived my love for music. His musical ability and way of thinking about music is unlike anyone I have ever come across. I am now studying Jonny and similarly to Nick studying everything he has done. From work he's done with Radiohead, film composing and The Smile. I analyse how he plays his instruments and why he chooses the chords and notes he does, his use of strings in majority of his music. I have books breaking down every bit of music theory in every song in all 9 Radiohead albums. I never realised you could break the rules of music that much until I started listening to Radiohead. Radiohead will forever be my favourite band. Jonny Greenwood, Thom Yorke, Colin Greenwood, Phil Selway and Ed O'Brien are the reason I will keep and never stop persuing music.
